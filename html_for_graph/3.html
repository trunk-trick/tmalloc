Each entry within fastbinsY[i] acts as a singly-linked list header, storing pointers to freed chunks of specific sizes (ranging from MINSIZE upwards with alignment constraints). Chunks are maintained in LIFO (Last-In, First-Out) order, terminating with a null pointer to designate the end of the bin segment.
